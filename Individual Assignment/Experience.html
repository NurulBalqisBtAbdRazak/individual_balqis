About My Experience

AS A UNIVERSITY STUDENT'S EXPERIENCE

I started my student life in University when I was 18 years old which is 2 years ago.

At first, I think it must be hard for me since I have never been away from my parents for long time. And yes, for the first week at college, I always faced a homesick so I called my mother everyday. Plus, I'm an introvert person, I'm not good at communicate with strangers too. Luckily, my ex classmate in high school become my roommate so she helped me a lot during that time.

In addition, I'm a thalasemia patient. So I need to see a doctor once every two months for treatment. I was worried at first since I still don't know how to manage it but my friends and lecturer assist me during that time. I was very grateful.

After that, I started to have a good relationship between my classmates. Even I'm not close towards everyone, but at least I have someone that I can talk with. For my first semester, everything run smoothly. I'm having a good time during that semester and also always walk with friends to get know about the area of my university. To be honest, my first semester is a time where I was very active in my students life since I've got very excited.

Actually, having an experienced as university's student is best thing and very useful for me. From an introvert person, now I already got more confidence rather than before. I also get to know more people. Before this, I just spend my time at home and rarely go out to meet people. But after that, I become more happy and confidence to talk with others.

My cousin only having a light injury at hand while me suffered an injury to the left face until it bled and swelled and my left hand is also broken. The owner of the car helped us to called for my parents.
